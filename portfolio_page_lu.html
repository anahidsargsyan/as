Product

Lu was my first Freelance project. The scope of the product was to do complete visual design and make sure the UI was appealing for the "sports" fanatique.

Solution

I created an app that reflectd the scoring on court in digital format. The dashboard uses the team colors for visual identification and tracking. I also did the logo design so that from first glance it was clear what the app was used for.

Logo Design

Design evolution

The app went through a few design evolutions, and the logo changed with the overall UI, staying true to the direction set by the app itself.

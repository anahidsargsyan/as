Category

App Store Link

iTunes Store

Challenge

Screenshots was a product that rose out of presonal need. Working as a UX designer I would take screenshots of a lot of apps. The trouble was I couldn't find a good way to keep, share or organize the screenshots on my iPhone.

Solution

The simple solution was an app that could easily filter through my photos and isolate the screenshots. The app would make it easy to delete, hide or share screenshots on my phone.

User Flow

User Flow

The user flow goes through the critical user flow. It shows the three major ways to experience the app: single browse, multi select, and filters. It also describes each screen in detail and shows where the analytics are needed most.

User research, compeitive space analysis, prototype, MVP, work with developer, product test, iteration, UX, UI, Visual design, ship product to App Store, user testing.
